Regex Validation Tests

This page tests all validation rules used in the ALU Life Planner form.

1. Title Validation

Rule: No spaces at the beginning or end

Regex: /^\S(?:.*\S)?$/

2. Duration Validation

Rule: Positive integers only (min = 1)

Regex: /^[1-9]\d*$/

3. Date Validation

Rule: Format must be YYYY-MM-DD (valid month & day range)

Regex: /^\d{4}-(0[1-9]|1[0-2])-(0[1-9]|[12]\d|3[01])$/

4. Tag Validation

Rule: Letters, spaces, and hyphens only (proper word structure)

Regex: /^[A-Za-z]+(?:[ -][A-Za-z]+)*$/

5. Duplicate Word Detection (Advanced)

Rule: Detect repeated words like "study study"

Regex: /\b(\w+)\s+\1\b/i
